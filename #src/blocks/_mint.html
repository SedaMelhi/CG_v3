How to mint?

How to mint the Medusa? Club Gorgon exists on Elrond blockchain. To mint your Medusa NFT membership pass you will need to:
  1. Download Maiar Wallet App and set up your Elrond wallet. Instructions if needed: www.maiar.com
  2. Buy EGLD via the Maiar App or use an exchange and send the EGLD to your Elrond wallet/Maiar App.
  3. Click the MINT button on our website and connect via the Maiar App to mint your membership Medusa NFT.

  4. You just minted your Medusa NFT!

    Enjoy all the benefits of being our clubs privileged member!
