About the project

The 80's marked the introduction of digital art into the mainstream with computers entering our homes. Today as NFTs take centre stage of a new digital art revolution and we aim to establish a Metaverse native and community centric ART-DAO, thus bringing together the worlds of digital and physical art in one place, creating a CURATED ART PLATFORM where selected artists' works will be airdropped to the members of Club Gorgon. Because of this we see ourselves as the go to phygital art club of the future.
